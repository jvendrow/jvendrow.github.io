I am a first-year PhD student at MIT EECS, where I am advised by Aleksander MÄ…dry.

I recently completed my BS double majoring in Computer Science and Math at UCLA, where I was advised by Deanna Needell and mentored by Jamie Haddock and Hanbaek Lyu.

Research

My research focuses on principled ways to make machine learning models safe and robust when deployed in the real world. During my undergrad, I worked on on applications of matrix and tensor factorization to topic modeling, computer vision, and network science.
